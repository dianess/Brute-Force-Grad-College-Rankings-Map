Project Summary

In this project, our team collected geoJSON data from Data.gov for Postsecondary Schools from the 2016-2017 school year. We also collected data from U.S. News & World Report for the top 20 graduate schools in each of 9 categories (Business, Law, Medicine, Engineering, Nursing, Education, Fine_Arts, Library and Information Studies, and Public_Affairs) and each of the 7 subcategories of science (Biological Sciences, Chemistry, Computer Science, Earth Sciences, Mathematics, Physics, and Statistics).

Using our geoJSON file, we plotted the universities on a U.S. map.
